Support Elder Care

Support Elder Care’s Sustaining Campaign:

The valuable programs that Elder Care provides rely on the generous donations of individuals. These donations make it possible to continue to provide care, support and wellness for seniors. Every contribution counts in continuing with our success of helping mature adults live happy, healthy, independent lives.

Help us continue to make a difference in the lives of families within our community. Your donation to Elder Care is tax deductible.

Elder Care is a 501(c)3 approved by the Internal Revenue Service and is also a Bartlesville Regional United Way agency.

2016 Annual Appeal

The services that Elder Care provides are made possible by the generous support of community minded individuals, just like you. Elder Care wouldn’t be able to help over 850 seniors, caregivers and their families each month without your assistance. As we celebrate our 33rd year of providing compassionate care and assistance to seniors, we find that our mission is as timely and pertinent today as it was over three decades ago. The number of seniors is growing faster than any other segment of our population nationwide and in our region the senior population is growing at an even greater pace.

We would very much appreciate your commitment to support our annual appeal. Your tax deductible donation will make an immediate difference in the lives of seniors in our area. Please know that we value our donor relationships greatly and that we will always work diligently to exercise good stewardship with the gifts that are entrusted to our care.

Elder Care’s Heritage Society

Join Elder Care’s Heritage Society for donors who have chosen to name Elder Care and/or the Elder Care Foundation in their will or estate plan. These gifts ensure the future of Elder Care’s important work in our community and each year we will honor these donors at a special reception. Members will also receive a certificate in recognition of their commitment to improving the lives of seniors and their caregivers.

The Golden Circle

The Golden Circle includes donors who make annual gifts of $1,000 or more to Elder Care and/or the Elder Care Foundation. Donors will receive special acknowledgement in Elder Care’s Prime Times newsletter (published monthly as an insert in the Bartlesville Examiner-Enterprise) and will be recognized at a special event each year.
