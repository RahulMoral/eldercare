Golden Opportunities

Join the Fun

Golden Opportunities (GO) was designed to enhance the overall quality of life for active seniors in the Bartlesville area. It offers fitness and educational classes, social events and day trips for any budget. Stop by, enjoy the fun and see why everyone is smiling. Don’t miss this opportunity to spread your horizons; become a member today.

Download a current copy of our Spring 2017 brochure.

Golden Opportunities Membership

Golden Opportunities Membership is optional, but the Members Only benefits pay for themselves throughout the year. By becoming a Member, you are also helping to sustain the longevity of this kind of program.

A Membership includes:

  • 20% off all GO classes
  • 10% off all GO travel
  • Exclusive Members Only Events
  • Good through December 31, 2017 Prorated Fee: $50/Individual, $80/Married Couple
Golden Hour

Golden Hour is a social night with live entertainment, complimentary food and beverages and giveaways held the fourth Thursday of every month at 5:30 p.m. No RSVP is required and it is open to anyone age 60 and over.

Benefits of Group Travel

There are many benefits of traveling as a group with Golden Opportunities. Traveling in a group is extremely cost effective since all expenses are shared and drastically reduced. Group travel provides the opportunity to see a maximum number of places incurring minimum expenditure. Group travelers are often favored and receive preferential treatment not offered for individual travelers.
